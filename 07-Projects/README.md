## üöÄ Getting Started
1.  **Framework**: Focus on **PyTorch**. It is the most common framework in research and modern AI companies.
2.  **Dataset**: Start with **MNIST** or **CIFAR-10**. They are small enough to train on a standard laptop CPU.
3.  **Visualization**: Use **TensorBoard** or **Weights & Biases** to plot your training and validation loss curves in real-time.
